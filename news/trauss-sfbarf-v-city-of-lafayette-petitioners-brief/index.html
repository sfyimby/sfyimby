Trauss, SFBARF v. City of Lafayette: Petitioners' Brief

Diego Aguilar-Canabal
Monday, November 28, 2016

Last year, our webmaster Sonja Trauss filed a lawsuit against the City of Lafayette, a wealthy East Bay suburb, for violating state housing law. The suit concerns Homes at Deer Hill, a development of 44 single-family homes estimated to sell for $1.2 million. The developer, O'Brien Homes, had previously proposed a 315-unit apartment complex at the same site, but was met with stiff local opposition. This article from San Francisco Magazine provides ample background, and the recently filed petitioners' brief is below.
