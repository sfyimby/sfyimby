Construction Site In Emeryville Burns Down
Jul 18, 2016
East Bay
EMERYVILLE, CA - In the early morning hours of Wednesday, July 6th, residents of the San Pablo Ave. & Adeline St. area on the Oakland-Emeryville border awoke to a massive fire that destroyed the wood frame of an apartment construction site, along with neighboring homes. The six-alarm fire spread to nearby townhomes and apartments, forcing residents to evacuate as windows buckled from the heat. Ten neighboring townhouses were damaged, and the project’s 100 units are indefinitely delayed. ... more
