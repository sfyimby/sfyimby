Popcorn For Planners: This Week's Best YIMBY Videos

Diego Aguilar Canabal
Wednesday, August 31, 2016

We started this news site with the wholly altruistic (pinkie promise) intentions of spreading far and wide our cheeky obsession with videos of local government hearings. It's easy for us to forget in the Bay Area that this top-notch prime-time viewing happens all over the country, even the rest of the non-American world. With pro-pedestrian, density-loving nerds extolling the entertainment value of #oakmtg and #berkmtg all over the Internet, we found it necessary to breathe some fresh air into this lurid subculture by showing some love for our friends in Minneapolis.
