South San Francisco Urges Housing Development Near Railroad
Aug 1, 2016
South San Francisco
Earlier this month, the mayor and city council of South San Francisco passed a resolution encouraging Union Pacific Railroad to cooperate with local government efforts on transit-oriented development projects near the future Caltrain station.
