Campos Calls for Another Construction Moratorium in The Mission
Aug 8, 2016
SF
Mission
Last week, District 9 Supervisor David Campos sent a letter to the San Francisco Planning Commission demanding a halt to three market-rate residential developments in the 24th Street corridor. Bolstered by the neighborhood business association Calle 24, Campos insisted that a pause was needed to study potential effects of development on displacement of local retail.
